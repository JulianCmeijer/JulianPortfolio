Design Proces

Onderzoek

Interviewed 12 small business owners to understand their financial workflows. Discovered that cash flow visualization and tax preparation were their biggest pain points.

Wireframes

Restructured the dashboard to prioritize high-level metrics while allowing deep dives into specific transactions. Focused on a modular design for scalability.

Visual design

Designed custom charts and graphs that simplify complex data. Used color theory to indicate financial health and urgent tasks effectively.

Testen

Built high-fidelity prototypes to test navigation and interaction patterns. Refined the transaction tagging system based on user feedback.
